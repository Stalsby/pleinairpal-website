Everything you need

Built for the outdoor artist

Every feature designed around how plein air painters actually work — spontaneous, location-driven, and community-focused.

Interactive Map

Discover paintings pinned to the exact spot they were created. Find inspiring locations and explore through other artists' eyes.

Paint Live

Share your real-time location while painting so fellow artists can find you. See who's out painting nearby and connect on the spot.

Social Feed

A feed of paintings from artists you follow, with likes and comments. Build genuine connections with the plein air community.

Events & Workshops

Find plein air competitions, paint-outs, and workshops. Host your own meetups and workshops directly in the app.

Getting started

Simple as picking
up a brush

01

Create your profile

Sign up in seconds. Add your bio, preferred mediums, and start following artists whose work inspires you.

02

Upload your paintings

Photograph your work, tag the location, add medium and size — your painting gets pinned to the map and shared with the community.

03

Explore & connect

Discover painting locations, find artists painting live near you, attend events, and build your plein air community.
